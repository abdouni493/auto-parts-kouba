## ✨ Feature Summary

### ✅ What's Implemented

1. **Store Assignment** - Assign workers to specific stores
2. **Worker Login** - Employees can log in with their accounts
3. **POS Lock** - Workers cannot change their assigned store
4. **Pricing Control** - Workers only see selling prices

### 📍 Where It Works

- **Employee Management** - Create/edit employees with store
- **POS System** - Store locked, pricing restricted
- **Worker Portal** - Limited to assigned store

### 🎯 Benefits

- Admins manage worker access by store
- Workers cannot accidentally change stores
- Cost information protected
- Clear separation of duties
